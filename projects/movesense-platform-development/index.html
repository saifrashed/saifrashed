Movesense platform development

View in App store Send email Bio

Delta Sport Innovation offers products and services in the field of sports and physical activity. They approached me with a request to design, implement, test, and release their Movesense-based mobile athlete activity tracking system.

Requirements

After numerous meetings, brainstorming sessions, and calls, we established a shared mental model for the solution's design. Movesense sensors can be used across various domains, such as medical, healthcare, and fitness. In this particular case, we concentrated on sports and fitness, using relevant formulas, metrics, and literature to develop the list of requirements. Once the list was finalized, we continued to the development phase.

App

Using Flutter, I developed both iOS and Android builds from a single codebase. Based on the requirements, the screens were designed and iteratively built. Features were implemented using an agile approach in two-week sprints, ensuring the client received a releasable MVP version after each sprint. Typically, sprints are conducted with teams using SCRUM, but this approach is equally effective for collaboration between a developer and a client, as it organizes the project efficiently.

Movesense offers a plugin for Flutter allowing to client app to connect to the sensors over bluetooth, requests to be sent from the client app to the sensors, also subscribe to values recorded by the specific metrics. However, there are many caveats in developing for these sensors, and Movesense documents well, but doesn't disclose all these issues.

Firmware

The Movesense documentation contains a diagram showing how the firmware, located on the sensor itself, and the client interact. The design of any application on the Movesense platform must take these components, as well as Bluetooth transmission, into account.

The platform allows the underlying base firmware to be updated while keeping the custom-built software intact. This is possible due to the use of REST interfaces, which hide any implementation details. The team also made a variety of of sample apps showcasing the capabilities of the sensor.
