Writespace

See repository Presentation

Writespace is a writing tool designed to improve the learning experience for students in the field of scientific writing. With this platform the process of writing and revising assignments, is made more engaging, both for teachers and students.

Kickin' it off

The goal was to organize ourselves, a diverse team of 11 people, in a functional DevOps team, practicing Agile principles.

We decided to divide the team into a front-end and back-end group. There was a Git master who acted as the scrum master, along with a lead for the front-end and back-end groups. Each group member was chosen by preferences, and leads were chosen according to their experience.

After deciding on the teams, we continued to the initial brainstorm session, especially since we had only a few weeks to build the solution. During this session, we tried to make sense of it all: What do front-end, back-end, and DevOps mean, and how do different platforms and technologies fit into this picture? Where do we agree and disagree on?

Gamification

Writespace gamifies by adding an element of fun and motivation to the learning process. Gamification elements are selected from a wide variety of sources. Students can earn points, badges, or rewards based on their progress, quality of writing, and adherence to assignment guidelines.

This gamified approach encourages healthy competition among students and boosts their engagement and commitment to the task.

Target audience

Writespace is a writing tool developed to improve the scientific writing experience for students. The platform tackles assignment drafting and revision, applying techniques to improve engagement and efficiency for both students and teachers.

Key requirements

  • Badge System: Students earn badges for participation and achievement in writing exercises and assignments.
  • Interactive Quizzes: Writing skill quizzes help students improve and provide opportunities to earn badges.
  • Teacher Interaction: Teachers grade assignments, provide feedback, and assign badges to recognize student progress.
  • Canvas Integration: Writespace integrates with Canvas, ensuring a familiar environment for students and educators.

Technical architecture

The system consists of three main components: client, service, and model. The client fetches data and displays it in the user interface. The service handles business logic. The model defines the underlying data structure.

Database Diagram

The model primarily follows this entity relationship diagram. Certain elements are excluded, as MongoDB allows document storage without separate tables. This structure was used to develop the API and does not include Canvas data.

Deployment Strategy

The system requires three servers for deployment: a Node.js server for hosting the Next.js client, a separate Express server for the API, and a MongoDB server for the database. Next.js runs a Node.js instance already, so why not build the API in there? We decided on a seperate API and reserve Next.js for UI only.

Conclusion

Writespace successfully improves the scientific writing experience by integrating engaging gamification elements that motivate students through badges and interactive quizzes. The university concurs, awarding a final grade of 9.
