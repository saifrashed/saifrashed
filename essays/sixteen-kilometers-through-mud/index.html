16 kilometers through mud

30 September, 2025

Shoes filled with mud, eyes covered with sand, overloaded cruciate ligaments, breathlessness, intensive calve cramps taking away your ability to keep going on. What if I told you that this is considered a form of recreation? Would you ridicule me? Let's say someone would offer you this delightful experience, surely you would decline, right? Well, lets just say that I decided not to do so and with two of my friends I did the Mudmasters obstacle run to do the 16 kilometers of hell.
